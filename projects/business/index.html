No Pittie Left Behind

Perfectly Paired Pitties (P3) is a volunteer-run rescue effort to save pitties and support veterans.

P3 loves Pitties and respects our Veterans. Thousands of Pitties are euthanized daily, while many Veterans face mental health struggles. P3 aims to change that.

Our Mission

P3 rescues Pit Bulls from overcrowded shelters, trains them as emotional support dogs, and pairs them with Veterans coping with PTSD.
